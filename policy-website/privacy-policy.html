Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

Developed by Conflux Solutions LLC

1. Introduction

Welcome to Phone Addiction Mugshot ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Camera Data: We access your device camera to capture selfie photos ("mugshots") as part of the app's core functionality
  • Screen Time Data: We access your Screen Time usage data to track phone usage and trigger mugshot reminders
  • Photo Library: We store captured mugshots locally on your device and may access your photo library if you choose to save images

2.2 Usage Data

Analytics Data: We collect anonymized usage statistics including:

  • App opens and session duration
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Screen time milestones reached
  • Crash reports and error logs

2.3 Device Information

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App version information

3. AI Disclosure (2026 Requirement)

This app does NOT use artificial intelligence or machine learning for data processing, decision-making, or content generation. All functionality is deterministic and rule-based:

  • Mugshot capture is manually triggered by the user or based on simple time thresholds
  • No AI analysis of photos or user behavior
  • No automated decision-making affecting users

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide core app functionality (mugshot capture and storage)
  • Track your screen time and send hourly reminders
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send important app updates and notifications
  • Provide customer support

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

  • All mugshot photos are stored locally on your device in the app's secure sandbox
  • We do NOT upload your photos to our servers or any cloud service
  • Screen time data remains on your device

5.2 Analytics Data

  • Anonymized usage analytics are transmitted to our analytics provider (PostHog/Mixpanel)
  • Analytics data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
  • No personally identifiable information is included in analytics

5.3 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • Secure local storage using iOS security features
  • Regular security audits and updates

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted analytics providers (PostHog/Mixpanel) under strict data processing agreements
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Data Access and Deletion

  • You can delete all mugshots directly within the app
  • You can request deletion of analytics data by contacting us at privacy@confluxsolutions.app
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

7.2 Permissions

  • You can revoke camera and Screen Time permissions at any time through iOS Settings
  • The app will not function properly without these permissions

7.3 Analytics Opt-Out

  • You can request opt-out from analytics tracking by contacting us

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the United States, please note that we process data in the United States. By using our app, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy within the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for material changes

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

12. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

EU/EEA residents have additional rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
