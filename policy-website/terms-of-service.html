3. Service Description

Phone Addiction Mugshot is a mobile application that:

  • Tracks your daily screen time using iOS Screen Time API
  • Prompts you to take selfie photos ("mugshots") after each hour of phone usage
  • Stores your mugshots locally on your device
  • Provides a gallery to view your collected mugshots
  • Enables sharing of mugshots to social media platforms
